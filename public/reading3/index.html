Topics in Algebraic Topology

A collaborative exploration of advanced topics in topology and geometry

Week 1 - October 15, 2024

Introduction to Spectral Sequences

We introduce the notion of spectral sequences and discuss their applications in computing homology and cohomology groups.

Week 2 - October 22, 2024

The Serre Spectral Sequence

An exploration of the Serre spectral sequence and its role in computing the homology of fiber bundles.

Week 3 - October 29, 2024

Applications to Homotopy Theory

We discuss applications of spectral sequences to problems in homotopy theory, including computations of homotopy groups of spheres.
