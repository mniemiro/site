Reading Seminar

Topics in Algebraic Topology

A collaborative exploration of advanced topics in topology and geometry

Week 1 - October 15, 2024

Introduction to Spectral Sequences

We introduce the notion of spectral sequences and discuss their applications in computing homology and cohomology groups.

Week 2 - October 22, 2024

The Serre Spectral Sequence

An exploration of the Serre spectral sequence and its role in computing the homology of fiber bundles.

Week 3 - October 29, 2024

Applications to Homotopy Theory

We discuss applications of spectral sequences to problems in homotopy theory, including computations of homotopy groups of spheres.

Week 4 - November 5, 2024

Cohomology Operations

An introduction to cohomology operations and their role in stable homotopy theory. We explore Steenrod squares and their applications.

Week 5 - November 12, 2024

K-Theory and Vector Bundles

We study topological K-theory and its relationship with vector bundles over manifolds. Applications to index theory and characteristic classes.

Week 6 - November 19, 2024

Cobordism Theory

An exploration of cobordism groups and their computation using spectral sequences. We discuss Thom's theorem and its implications.

Week 7 - November 26, 2024

Characteristic Classes

We study Chern classes, Stiefel-Whitney classes, and Pontryagin classes. Applications to classification of vector bundles and manifolds.

Week 8 - December 3, 2024

Fiber Bundles and Fibrations

An in-depth look at fiber bundles, principal bundles, and their role in topology. We explore the long exact sequence of a fibration.

Week 9 - December 10, 2024

Homotopy Groups of Spheres

Computational techniques for homotopy groups of spheres using spectral sequences. Discussion of stable and unstable homotopy groups.

Week 10 - December 17, 2024

Differential Forms and de Rham Cohomology

Connections between differential geometry and algebraic topology. The de Rham theorem and its applications.

Week 11 - December 24, 2024

Atiyah-Singer Index Theorem

An introduction to the Atiyah-Singer index theorem and its significance in connecting analysis, geometry, and topology.

Week 12 - December 31, 2024

Elliptic Cohomology

We explore elliptic cohomology theories and their relationship with modular forms and string theory.
